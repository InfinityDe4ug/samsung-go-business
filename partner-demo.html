What Is Samsung Go Business?

A Galaxy-native fintech platform that employees can elect as their direct-deposit destination through your payroll system. Workers choose Samsung Go the same way they'd select any bank—zero friction, zero change to your payroll process.

Employer Payroll System ↓ Your Platform (existing) ↓ ACH Origination (no change) ↓ Sponsor Bank ← Samsung Go Account ↓ Employee's Galaxy Device ├─ Instant balance ├─ Split deposits ├─ Mobile check deposit └─ Native Galaxy payments

How The Integration Works

Step 1: Add Samsung Go to Your UI

Add a direct-deposit election option in your employee onboarding or benefits portal. Same fields as any bank account (routing #, account #, confirmation).

Step 2: Route ACH as Normal

When an employee elects Samsung Go, route their paycheck to our sponsor bank's routing number. No special formatting, no new fields. Existing ACH infrastructure handles it exactly like any other account.

Step 3: Monitor & Report

Dashboard visibility: How many employees elected Samsung Go, aggregate volume, anonymized insights. No ongoing reconciliation—our sponsor bank handles settlement.

Integration Timeline:

Development: 4–6 weeks | Sandbox testing: 2–4 weeks | Go-live: 1–2 weeks
